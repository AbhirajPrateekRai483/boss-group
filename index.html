Our Companies

Boss Group is building innovative companies across multiple industries.

Alzia

Alzia is an advanced AI, software, website, app, and technology company operating under Boss Group. It focuses on building innovative digital products and next-generation solutions.

Zonda

Zonda is the gaming company of Boss Group. It develops immersive games and interactive entertainment experiences, including titles such as Zonda Cricket and Zonda Football.

Raiza

Raiza is the social media company of Boss Group. It creates high-quality digital content, builds trusted online communities, and connects millions of people across multiple social media platforms.

Robox

Robox is the robotics and manufacturing company of Boss Group. It focuses on designing advanced robots and intelligent systems powered by Artificial Intelligence and Robotics.
